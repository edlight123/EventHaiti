## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit EventHaiti**: Navigate to the homepage at your EventHaiti URL
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Located in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
3. **Choose Your Role**:
   - **Attendee**: Browse and buy tickets for events
   - **Organizer**: Create and manage your own events
4. **Fill in Your Details**:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
5. **Click "Create Account"**: You'll receive a verification email

### Logging In

1. Click **"Sign In"** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **"Sign In"** to access your account

## For Attendees

### Discovering Events

#### Homepage
The homepage displays all upcoming published events with powerful discovery tools:

**Browse Options:**
- **Search Bar**: Search by event name, venue, or description
- **Category Grid**: Click any category icon to filter (Music, Sports, Arts & Culture, etc.)
- **Advanced Filters**: Click the filter button to access:
  - Category selection
  - City/location filter
  - Date range (from/to dates)
  - Price range (min/max)
  - Sort options (by date, price, popularity)

#### Discover Page
Navigate to **/discover** for curated event recommendations:
- **Trending Events**: Most popular events based on ticket sales
- **Nearby Events**: Events in your selected location (default: Port-au-Prince)
- **Category Quick Links**: Browse all 10 event categories

#### Categories Page
Navigate to **/categories** to:
- View all event categories in a horizontal scrollable grid
- Click any category to see all events in that category
- All 10 categories displayed: Music, Sports, Arts & Culture, Business, Food & Drink, Community, Education, Technology, Health & Wellness, Other

### Viewing Event Details

Click any event card to view full details:
- Event banner image
- Title and category badge
- Full description
- Date, time, and duration
- Venue name and full address with map
- Ticket pricing and availability
- Remaining ticket count
- "Buy Ticket" button (requires login)

### Purchasing Tickets

#### Standard Tickets

1. **Click "Buy Ticket"** on an event detail page
2. **Select Quantity**: Choose how many tickets you need
3. **Apply Promo Code** (optional): Enter code if you have one
4. **Choose Payment Method**:
   - Credit/Debit Card (Stripe)
   - MonCash (Haitian mobile money)
5. **Complete Payment**: Follow the payment flow
6. **Confirmation**: You'll receive:
   - Email confirmation with ticket details
   - QR code for event entry
   - Access to ticket in "My Tickets"

#### Tiered Tickets

Some events offer multiple ticket tiers (VIP, General Admission, Early Bird):

1. **View Available Tiers**: Displayed with individual pricing
2. **Check Sales Windows**: Some tiers have limited availability periods
3. **Select Your Tier**: Choose the tier that fits your needs
4. **Proceed to Checkout**: Follow standard purchase flow

#### Group Discounts

Events may offer quantity-based discounts:
- **Automatic Application**: Discounts apply based on quantity selected
- **Discount Display**: Orange box shows applicable discount (e.g., "10% off for 5+ tickets")
- **Final Price**: Discount reflected in total before payment

#### Free Events

For free events:
1. **Click "Claim Free Ticket"** on the event page
2. **Instant Confirmation**: Ticket added to your account immediately
3. **QR Code Generated**: Access your ticket in "My Tickets"

### Managing Your Tickets

#### My Tickets Page

Access via **"My Tickets"** in the navigation bar to view:
- All your purchased and claimed tickets
- Event details for each ticket
- Ticket status (active, transferred, checked-in)
- Quick actions (view QR, transfer, request refund)

#### Viewing Ticket Details

Click any ticket card to view:
- Complete event information
- Your unique QR code for entry
- Ticket ID and purchase details
- Transfer and refund options

#### QR Code for Entry

**Important:** Your QR code is your ticket!

- **Display at Event**: Show QR code to event staff for scanning
- **Screenshot Option**: Save QR code image for offline access
- **One-Time Use**: QR code is invalidated after check-in
- **No Transfer After Check-In**: Cannot transfer checked-in tickets

### Transferring Tickets

EventHaiti allows you to transfer tickets to others with a 24-hour claim window.

#### How to Transfer a Ticket

1. **Go to Ticket Details**: Click the ticket you want to transfer
2. **Click "Transfer Ticket"** button
3. **Enter Transfer Details**:
   - Recipient's email address
   - Optional message explaining the transfer
4. **Submit Transfer Request**
5. **Share Transfer Link**: You'll receive:
   - A unique transfer link (valid for 24 hours)
   - Copy link button
   - WhatsApp share button
   - SMS share button

#### Transfer Link Options

After creating a transfer:
- **Copy Link**: Share via any messaging app or email
- **WhatsApp**: Direct share to WhatsApp with pre-filled message
- **SMS**: Send via text message to recipient's phone

#### What Happens Next

**For the Sender:**
- Original ticket marked as "Transfer Pending"
- Cannot attend event while transfer is pending
- Receives email when transfer is accepted or expires

**For the Recipient:**
- Receives email with transfer link
- Link valid for 24 hours from transfer creation
- Can accept or reject the transfer

#### Accepting a Transfer

As a recipient:
1. **Click Transfer Link**: From email or shared message
2. **View Transfer Details**:
   - Event information
   - Sender's name and message
   - Expiry countdown (24 hours)
3. **Choose Action**:
   - **Accept**: Ticket appears in your "My Tickets" immediately
   - **Reject**: Transfer cancelled, ticket returns to sender

**What Happens on Accept:**
- Ticket ownership transfers to you
- Your account becomes the new owner
- Original owner loses access to ticket
- You can now use the QR code for entry
- Both parties receive email confirmation

#### Transfer Restrictions

- **24-Hour Expiry**: Transfer links expire 24 hours after creation
- **Valid Tickets Only**: Can only transfer active, unchecked tickets
- **One Pending Transfer**: Cannot create multiple pending transfers for same ticket
- **No Transfer After Check-In**: Checked-in tickets cannot be transferred

### Requesting Refunds

EventHaiti offers refund requests for eligible tickets:

1. **Go to Ticket Details**: Navigate to the ticket you want to refund
2. **Click "Request Refund"**
3. **Provide Reason**: Explain why you're requesting a refund
4. **Submit Request**: Organizer will review
5. **Wait for Response**: Organizer approves or denies
6. **Refund Processing**: If approved, refund issued to original payment method

**Refund Eligibility:**
- Must be before event start time
- Organizer discretion on approval
- Refund policy displayed on event page

### Social Features

#### Favoriting Events

- **Click Heart Icon**: On any event card or detail page
- **View Favorites**: Access via **"Favorites"** page
- **Quick Access**: Easily find events you're interested in

#### Following Organizers

- **Follow Button**: On organizer profile or event pages
- **Updates**: Get notified of new events from followed organizers
- **Manage Follows**: View all followed organizers in profile

#### Event Reviews

After attending an event:
1. **Submit Review**: Rate and review on event page
2. **5-Star Rating**: Share your experience
3. **Help Others**: Your review helps attendees make decisions

## For Organizers

### Organizer Verification

**Required Before Creating Events**

EventHaiti requires organizer verification to maintain platform trust and safety.

#### Verification Process

1. **Navigate to Organizer Dashboard**: Click **"My Events"** in navigation
2. **Click "Verify Your Account"** (if not verified)
3. **Submit Verification Request**:
   - Government-issued ID photo (clear, readable)
   - Selfie with ID (for identity confirmation)
   - Organization details (optional)
4. **Wait for Review**: 24-48 hours typically
5. **Email Notification**: Receive approval or request for better photos
6. **Create Events**: Access unlocked after approval

#### Verification Statuses

- **Unverified**: Cannot create events, must submit verification
- **Pending**: Request under review (24-48 hours)
- **Approved**: Can create and manage events
- **Rejected**: Resubmit with clearer photos

### Creating an Event

Once verified:

1. **Go to "My Events"** in navigation
2. **Click "Create New Event"** button
3. **Fill in Event Details**:

#### Basic Information
- **Event Title**: Clear, descriptive name
- **Category**: Choose from 10 categories
- **Description**: Full event details (supports markdown)
- **Event Image**: Upload banner (recommended: 1200x600px)

#### Date & Time
- **Start Date/Time**: When event begins
- **End Date/Time**: When event ends
- **Time Zone**: Automatic based on location

#### Location
- **Venue Name**: Name of the location
- **Full Address**: Street address
- **City**: Select from Haitian cities
- **Coordinates** (optional): For map display

#### Tickets & Pricing
- **Ticket Price**: Set price or mark as free
- **Total Tickets**: Maximum capacity
- **Ticket Sales Dates** (optional): Early bird/late sales windows

#### Advanced Options
- **Promo Codes**: Create discount codes
- **Ticket Tiers**: VIP, General Admission, etc.
- **Group Discounts**: Quantity-based discounts
- **Recurring Events**: Weekly/monthly recurring
- **Virtual Events**: Online events with meeting links

4. **Preview Event**: Review all details
5. **Publish**: Make event live on platform

### Ticket Tiers

Offer multiple ticket types for your event:

#### Creating Tiers

1. **Go to Event Management** page for your event
2. **Click "Manage Ticket Tiers"**
3. **Add Tier**:
   - Tier Name (e.g., "VIP", "Early Bird", "General Admission")
   - Description of tier benefits
   - Price
   - Quantity available
   - Sales start/end dates (optional)
4. **Save Tier**: Now visible on event page

**Example Tiers:**
- **Early Bird**: HTG 500 (first 50 tickets, sales end 1 week before event)
- **General Admission**: HTG 750 (200 tickets)
- **VIP**: HTG 1500 (20 tickets with backstage access)

### Promo Codes

Create discount codes for marketing:

#### Setting Up Promo Codes

1. **Go to Event Management**
2. **Click "Promo Codes"**
3. **Create Code**:
   - Code Name (e.g., "EARLYBIRD2024")
   - Discount Type: Percentage or Fixed Amount
   - Discount Value
   - Usage Limit (total uses allowed)
   - Expiry Date
4. **Share Code**: Distribute to your audience

**Best Practices:**
- Keep codes simple and memorable
- Set reasonable expiry dates
- Monitor usage in analytics
- Use for marketing campaigns

### Group Discounts

Encourage larger ticket purchases:

#### Creating Group Discounts

1. **Go to Event Management**
2. **Click "Group Discounts"**
3. **Add Discount**:
   - Minimum Quantity (e.g., 5 tickets)
   - Discount Percentage (e.g., 10%)
   - Make active/inactive
4. **Automatic Application**: Discount applies at checkout when quantity threshold met

**Example:**
- Buy 5-9 tickets: 10% off
- Buy 10+ tickets: 20% off

### Recurring Events

Perfect for weekly or monthly events:

1. **Event Creation**: Check "Recurring Event"
2. **Set Recurrence Pattern**:
   - Frequency: Daily, Weekly, Monthly
   - End Date or Number of Occurrences
3. **Generate Instances**: System creates multiple event instances
4. **Manage Individually**: Each instance can be edited separately

### Virtual Events

Host online events:

1. **Event Creation**: Select "Virtual Event" option
2. **Add Meeting Details**:
   - Platform (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.)
   - Meeting Link
   - Access Instructions
3. **Ticket Purchase**: Attendees receive link via email
4. **Access**: Link displayed on ticket details page

Navigate to **"My Events"** to see:
- All your created events
- Event status (draft, published, past)
- Quick stats (tickets sold, revenue)
- Edit and manage options

#### Editing Events

1. **Click Event Card** in "My Events"
2. **Click "Edit Event"**
3. **Update Details**: Modify any event information
4. **Save Changes**: Updates appear immediately
5. **Notify Attendees** (optional): Email update to ticket holders

#### Event Analytics

View detailed analytics for each event:
- **Total Tickets Sold**: Current sales count

- **Sales Trend**: Chart of sales over time
- **Ticket Status**: Active, transferred, checked-in, refunded
- **Attendee List**: View all ticket holders

#### Exporting Attendees

1. **Go to Event Analytics**
2. **Click "Export Attendees"**

### Scanning Tickets at Your Event

EventHaiti provides a built-in QR scanner:

#### Using the Scanner

1. **Navigate to "Scan Tickets"** in organizer menu
2. **Select Your Event** from dropdown
3. **Allow Camera Access**: Browser will request permission
4. **Scan QR Codes**: Point camera at attendee's QR code
5. **Instant Verification**:
   - ‚úÖ **Valid**: Green checkmark, attendee admitted
   - ‚ùå **Invalid**: Red X with reason (already checked in, wrong event, etc.)
6. **Check-In Recorded**: Ticket marked as checked-in in system

#### Scanner Features

- **Offline Capability**: Works without constant internet (syncs when online)
- **Duplicate Prevention**: Cannot check in same ticket twice
- **Real-time Stats**: See check-in count during event
- **Manual Override**: Enter ticket ID manually if QR unreadable

### Managing Refund Requests

When attendees request refunds:

1. **View Pending Requests**: In event management dashboard
2. **Review Request Details**:
   - Attendee information
   - Reason for refund
   - Ticket details
3. **Approve or Deny**:
   - **Approve**: Refund processed, ticket invalidated
   - **Deny**: Provide reason, ticket remains valid
4. **Confirmation**: Attendee notified of decision

**Refund Best Practices:**
- Set clear refund policy on event page
- Be reasonable with legitimate requests
- Respond promptly (within 24-48 hours)
- Consider partial refunds for late cancellations

### Event Photos & Gallery

Share event photos with attendees:

1. **Upload Photos**: During or after event
2. **Photo Gallery**: Displayed on event page
3. **Attendee Access**: Ticket holders can view and download
4. **Marketing**: Use photos for future event promotion

### Common Issues

**Can't Find Ticket Transfer Link?**
- Check "My Tickets" page
- Look for "Transfer Pending" status
- Link sent to email used at registration

**QR Code Not Displaying?**
- Refresh the ticket page
- Check internet connection
- Screenshot backup available

**Payment Failed?**
- Verify card details
- Check sufficient funds
- Try alternative payment method (MonCash)
- Contact payment provider if issue persists

**Transfer Link Expired?**
- Transfers expire after 24 hours
- Original owner can create new transfer
- Contact sender for new transfer link

**Event Not Showing After Purchase?**
- Check "My Tickets" page (requires login)
- Verify payment completed
- Check spam folder for confirmation email
- Allow up to 5 minutes for processing
